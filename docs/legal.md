# Legal & Compliance

MAGMA Protocol is operated by MAGMA Protocol Corp, a Cayman Islands Segregated Portfolio Company. The protocol is designed to operate across jurisdictions while complying with applicable regulatory requirements.

## Restricted Jurisdictions

MAGMA Protocol is not available to residents or citizens of the following jurisdictions:

- United States of America (including all territories and possessions)
- United Kingdom
- Iran
- North Korea
- Cuba
- Syria
- Russia
- Belarus
- Crimea, Donetsk, and Luhansk regions
- Any other jurisdiction where use of the protocol would violate applicable law

Geographic blocking is implemented at the network level. Users in restricted jurisdictions are redirected to a blocking page and cannot access the protocol.

## Anti-Sybil

MAGMA Protocol requires anti-Sybil verification to maintain the integrity of the Conviction Score system. Each wallet must represent a unique participant. The protocol currently uses Gitcoin Passport for identity verification, with Solana Attestation Service (SAS) planned as a post-mainnet upgrade.
