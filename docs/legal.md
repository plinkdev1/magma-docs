# Legal & Compliance


MAGMA Protocol Corp is incorporated in the Cayman Islands as a Special Purpose Company through MetaLeX. The protocol is designed to operate in a regulatory environment that continues to evolve. The following positions reflect current design intent and are subject to change as regulatory clarity develops.


$MAGMA is a utility token. Nothing in this whitepaper constitutes an offer of securities, investment advice, or a guarantee of returns. Participation in MAGMA Protocol involves material risk, including the risk of total loss of committed capital on FALSE-resolving narratives.


MAGMA Protocol maintains full Terms and Conditions (MAGMA_TERMS_V1.md) covering participant rights, oracle dispute procedures, fee structures, and limitations of liability. Terms are presented to all participants during onboarding and must be accepted before participation.


Anti-Sybil verification is required for all participants via Civic Auth or Gitcoin Passport, ensuring that each participating wallet represents a unique human actor. This is a protocol requirement, not merely a compliance measure — the Conviction Score's integrity depends on it representing a single participant's prediction history.
