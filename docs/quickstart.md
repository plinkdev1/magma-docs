# Quickstart Guide

Get started with MAGMA Protocol in under 5 minutes.

## What You Need

- A Solana wallet (Phantom or Solflare recommended)
- SOL for transaction fees (less than 0.01 SOL)
- Capital you want to put to work (any supported token)

## Step 1 — Connect Your Wallet

Open the MAGMA app and tap **Connect Wallet**. MAGMA supports Phantom, Solflare, and any Mobile Wallet Adapter compatible wallet on Seeker.

Your wallet address becomes your identity on MAGMA. No username or email required.

## Step 2 — Accept the Terms

On first launch, MAGMA will ask you to confirm that you are not a resident of a restricted jurisdiction and that you understand this is experimental software. Read the terms carefully — by continuing you accept them.

## Step 3 — Find a Narrative

Browse the Feed. Narratives are predictions submitted by community members — things like:

- *'SOL will reach $300 before June 1'*
- *'Team X will win the championship'*
- *'This protocol will reach $1B TVL within 90 days'*

Each narrative shows:
- **Resolution date** — when the oracle resolves it TRUE or FALSE
- **Total SOL backed** — how much capital is already committed
- **Discovery window** — how much time is left to earn the early backer bonus

## Step 4 — Back a Narrative

Tap a narrative you believe in. Choose your token and amount. Your capital is immediately deployed to a DeFi yield protocol (Kamino, Meteora, or Save Finance) where it starts earning yield.

You earn yield regardless of whether the narrative resolves TRUE or FALSE. If you are correct, you also receive a yield multiplier based on your Conviction Score.

## Step 5 — Wait for Resolution

At the deadline, MAGMA's oracle resolves the narrative:

- **TRUE** — you receive your principal back plus multiplied yield plus your share of the Echo Pool
- **FALSE** — you receive your principal back plus base yield. 35% of your yield goes to the Echo Pool for correct backers

You never lose your principal. Only yield is redistributed.

## Step 6 — Build Your Conviction Score

Every correct backing adds +10 points to your Conviction Score. Higher scores unlock higher yield multipliers:

| Score | Tier | Yield Multiplier |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0-99 | Ember | 1.1x |
| 100-299 | Flare | 1.3x |
| 300-599 | Magma | 1.6x |
| 600-899 | Core | 2.0x |
| 900+ | Volcanic | 2.5x |

Your score is non-transferable and compounds over time. The longer you participate accurately, the better your returns on every future position.

## Step 7 — Submit Your Own Narratives

Have a strong conviction about something? Submit it as a narrative. Use the AI polish feature to make it specific and resolvable. If others back it and it resolves TRUE, you earn Creator Pool rewards.

## Tips for New Users

- **Back early** — the Discovery Multiplier rewards backing in the first 10% of a narrative's window with 2.0x yield
- **Be specific** — vague narratives get rejected by the oracle. 'SOL will go up' fails. 'SOL will close above $250 on June 1' resolves cleanly
- **Build streaks** — consecutive correct backings add a Streak Multiplier on top of everything else
- **Use SKR on Seeker** — SKR earns Guardian staking rewards while locked in any position, effectively doubling your yield source
