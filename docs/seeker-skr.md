# Seeker Phone & SKR Integration


The Seeker phone, produced by Solana Mobile, ships with the SKR token as its native ecosystem currency. As of March 2026, over 150,000 Seeker units have been distributed to holders, representing one of the largest existing communities of on-device crypto holders who have not yet had a dedicated conviction market application.


MAGMA is designed specifically for this audience. SKR is supported as a backing token, routing through the Guardian staking system — a Seeker-native yield mechanism that earns staking rewards on committed SKR without requiring separate delegation. When a Seeker holder backs a narrative with SKR, they earn Guardian staking rewards and narrative yield simultaneously on the same capital. This is unavailable through any other application.


SKR holders who stake SKR within MAGMA before the $MAGMA TGE snapshot receive a dedicated allocation from the $MAGMA ecosystem bucket (2–3% of total supply), weighted by stake amount and duration. This converts the most engaged Seeker community members into $MAGMA token holders at TGE — creating alignment between the Seeker ecosystem and MAGMA's long-term success.
