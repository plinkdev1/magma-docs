## Usage Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use the provided files without modification
- Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "M" letterform
- Use orange variant on white/light backgrounds
- Use white or dark variant on colored backgrounds

**Do not:**
- Stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
- Change the colors of the logo
- Place the logo on backgrounds that reduce contrast
- Use the wordmark at sizes below 80px wide
