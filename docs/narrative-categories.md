# Narrative Categories


MAGMA supports eleven narrative categories across two oracle modes. Standard Oracle categories are resolved by weighted data source consensus. Consensus Oracle categories require the four-signal aggregation described above.


## 8.1 Standard Oracle Categories

- MARKET: Crypto asset prices, DeFi metrics, on-chain statistics. Primary source: Pyth Network price feeds.
- ECOSYSTEM: Protocol TVL, user growth, on-chain activity metrics. Primary sources: DeFiLlama, Helius.
- SPORTS: Match outcomes, championships, player statistics. Primary sources: Odds API, TheSports.com.
- ESPORTS: Tournament results, league standings, competitive outcomes. Primary source: PandaScore.
- LEGAL: Court rulings, regulatory decisions, legislative outcomes. Primary sources: official legal databases.
- POLITICAL: Electoral results, policy implementations, official announcements. Primary sources: government sources, AP/Reuters.
- SCIENTIFIC: Research findings, clinical trial results, academic publications. Primary sources: published journals.
- GEOPOLITICAL: International agreements, diplomatic outcomes, treaty status. Primary sources: official government sources.

## 8.2 Consensus Oracle Categories

- SOCIAL: Social movement trajectories, platform growth, community developments. Four-signal consensus resolution.
- CULTURAL: Music, art, entertainment, meme adoption, cultural shift narratives. Four-signal consensus resolution.
- CONVICTION: Ideological claims, technology adoption theses, societal change predictions. Four-signal consensus resolution.

Consensus categories are the protocol's most distinctive offering. No existing prediction market platform handles cultural or ideological narratives at scale. These categories are also the most viral — they tap into existing community debates and give participants a financial stake in cultural outcomes, producing organic sharing and word-of-mouth growth that empirical categories do not.
