# Token Economics


## 9.1 Protocol Revenue


MAGMA generates revenue from eight streams, activating in phases as the protocol matures:

- Protocol fee on TRUE resolution: 2% of total backing pool. Active from launch.
- Yield spread on DeFi deposits: MAGMA routes committed capital to lending protocols at a slight spread below the raw protocol APY. Active from launch.
- FALSE resolution treasury allocation: 40% of FALSE resolution capital. Active from launch.
- Narrative submission fees: 0.01 SOL per submission (waived for $MAGMA stakers). Spam prevention, secondary revenue.
- SideShift commission: 0.5% of all token swaps conducted through the MAGMA interface.
- Deposit facilitation fee: APY spread on protocol-facilitated DeFi deposits through the MAGMA app.
- $MAGMA staker revenue share (post-TGE): 40% of treasury revenue distributed monthly to stakers.
- Oracle infrastructure licensing (post-Beta): B2B fee for other protocols using MAGMA's oracle system.

## 9.2 $MAGMA Token


$MAGMA is a utility and governance token that amplifies returns within the protocol. It does not replace the Conviction Score — the two systems are additive. $MAGMA launches after the protocol has demonstrated product-market fit on mainnet.


Total supply: 500,000,000 — fixed, no inflation.


Allocation:

- Community ICO (futard.io): 35% — 175,000,000 tokens. Immediate unlock.
- Protocol Treasury: 25% — 125,000,000. MetaDAO futarchy governed.
- Team / MAGMA Corp: 18% — 90,000,000. Performance-gated vesting.
- Ecosystem and Partners: 12% — 60,000,000. Pyth, Arcium, Helius, Fogo, Superclaw.
- Protocol Liquidity: 6% — 30,000,000. Meteora DLMM and Fogo DEX.
- Advisors: 4% — 20,000,000. Six-month cliff, eighteen-month vest.

Team vesting is performance-gated at five price tranches (2×, 4×, 8×, 16×, 32× ICO price measured by 90-day TWAP). No time-based unlock exists. Team tokens unlock only when the protocol achieves verifiable price milestones.

## 9.4 Buyback Mechanism


Weekly automated $MAGMA purchases are funded by 20% of protocol treasury allocation derived from FALSE resolution capital. Purchased $MAGMA is distributed to Tier 3+ stakers as bonus distributions. This creates a reflexive loop: more FALSE resolutions → more buyback pressure → higher $MAGMA price → more valuable to stake → more staking → more utility.
